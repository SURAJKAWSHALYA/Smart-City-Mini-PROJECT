Local Issues




Garbage Problem

Uncollected Waste in Streets --Garbage is often left on roads and in public places without being collected. This makes the city look dirty and can block drains during rain.

Lack of Bins and Recycling --There aren’t enough dustbins in many areas, so people throw waste on the ground. Recycling is also not common, which increases pollution.

Health and Environment Issues --Open garbage attracts flies, mosquitoes, and stray animals. It causes bad smells, spreads diseases, and harms the environment.









Health Problems

Air Pollution --Smoke from vehicles, factories, and burning garbage makes the air dirty. Breathing this polluted air can cause coughing, asthma, and other lung problems.

Poor Sanitation --Some areas don’t have clean toilets or proper drainage systems. This can lead to the spread of diseases like dengue, diarrhea, and skin infections.

Lack of Public Health Awareness --Many people don’t know how to stay healthy or prevent illness. Without health education, basic habits like washing hands or using clean water are often ignored.

Education Issues

Limited access to digital learning --Many students do not have internet or devices at home. This makes it hard for them to join online classes or use digital learning tools.

Outdated Classrooms --Some schools still use old teaching methods and lack modern equipment. Broken furniture and poor classroom conditions affect how well students learn.

Unequal Quality of Education --Students in rural or low-income areas often get a lower quality of education. They have fewer resources, less experienced teachers, and limited support.

Subsurface Facility Problems

Outdated Water Pipelines and Drainage --Many cities still use old underground water and drainage systems. These pipelines often leak or break, wasting water and causing floods during rain.

Poor Underground Mapping --There is little information about where pipes and cables are buried. Because of this, workers often damage them while digging, leading to more problems.

Frequent Road Repairs --Due to weak underground planning, roads are dug up often to fix hidden issues. This causes traffic, dust, and delays in daily life for residents.
